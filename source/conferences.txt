+ The First International Workshop on Combinatorial Reconfiguration (CoRe 2015)

The First International Workshop on Combinatorial Reconfiguration (CoRe 2015) was held on February 18-20, 2015 in Sendai, Japan.
This is the first workshop dedicated to the topic of "combinatorial reconfiguration."
The main goal of this workshop is to foster new collaborations between participants who have common interest in reconfiguration and related problems.
For more information, please see the [http://www.ecei.tohoku.ac.jp/alg/core2015/ conference's website].
